Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025
Effective Date: August 2025

Scope: This Privacy Policy describes how Trontiq (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use the Trontiq Chrome browser extension.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account: Email address, display name, full name (when you create an account).
  • Resume/Profile (optional): Resume text and extracted profile fields you choose to save.
  • Subscription: Status and plan details. Card data is handled by Stripe; we do not store card numbers.

Usage & Technical Data

  • Extension state: authentication state flags, UI preferences, language.
  • Performance/caching: subscription cache metadata (e.g., status, usage counts, timestamps).
  • Diagnostics: basic logs, extension version, browser info for troubleshooting.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core extension functionality and personalized assistance.
  • Authenticate users and manage subscriptions.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and support.
We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share data with service providers necessary to operate the extension (e.g., Stripe for payments).

Data Security

  • HTTPS for data in transit; encryption at rest on our servers.
  • Session tokens stored in HttpOnly cookies (not in local storage).
  • Principle of least privilege and periodic security reviews.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Access/Update: Manage profile data in the extension.
  • Delete: Use in-app delete (if available) or email us to delete your account and associated data.
  • Export: Email us to request an export of your data (available upon request).
  • Sign out: You can sign out at any time; local cached state will be cleared.

Children’s Privacy

The extension is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and revise the “Last Updated” date.
