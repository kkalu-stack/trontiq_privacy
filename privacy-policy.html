Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

Introduction

Trontiq ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and full name when you create an account
  • Resume Data: Resume text and extracted profile information (stored securely on our servers)
  • Subscription Information: Payment details and subscription status (processed securely via Stripe)

Usage Data

  • Extension Usage: Authentication state, UI preferences, and language settings
  • Performance Data: Subscription cache data (expires after 15 minutes)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version, and error logs

How We Store Your Data

Data Type Storage Location Security Level Retention Period
Session Tokens HttpOnly Cookies High (Not accessible to JavaScript) Until logout or session expiry
Authentication State Chrome Storage Local Medium (Extension context only) Until logout
User Profile & Resume Secure Server (Supabase) High (Encrypted at rest) Until account deletion
Subscription Cache Chrome Storage Local Medium (Extension context only) 15 minutes (auto-expires)
Payment Information Stripe (PCI compliant) High (Industry standard) As required by law

How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account
  • Service Provision: To provide AI-powered job application assistance
  • Cross-Device Sync: To sync your profile across different devices
  • Performance Optimization: To cache data for faster response times
  • Customer Support: To provide technical support and resolve issues
Important: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used solely for providing our service.

Data Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest
  • Session Management: JWT tokens are stored in HttpOnly cookies to prevent XSS attacks
  • Minimal Permissions: Extension requests only essential permissions
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update security measures

Your Rights and Controls

  • Access: You can view your data in the extension's Profile tab
  • Update: You can modify your profile information at any time
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out: You can sign out and clear local data

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Cache Data: Automatically expires after 15 minutes
  • Session Data: Cleared when you sign out
  • Payment Data: Retained as required by law and payment processors

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI compliant)
  • OpenAI: AI services for job application assistance

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
